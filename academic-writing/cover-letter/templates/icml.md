# ICML Cover Letter

> ICML does not use a cover letter for the main track — submission is through OpenReview structured forms (author list, abstract, checklists), not a free-form letter. Use this snapshot only when a cover letter is explicitly required (some workshops, special calls) or when the authors choose to provide one. ICML mirrors NeurIPS for those expectations.

## Tone

- Contribution-led, concise.

## Opening

- One sentence stating the algorithmic or theoretical contribution.
- Name the track.

## Contribution Framing

- Three concise contributions.
- One quantitative anchor traceable to the manuscript.
- For theoretical results: state assumptions briefly.

## Journal Fit

- Two sentences connecting the contribution to the ICML scope.

## Declarations (Required for ICML)

- Originality and dual-submission compliance.
- Reproducibility statement confirmation.

## Length

- Hard ceiling: 400 words.

## Common Desk-Reject Triggers

- Dual-submission violation.
- Theoretical contribution with hidden or unstated assumptions.
